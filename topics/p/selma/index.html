Selma Lagerlöf

Selma Lagerlöf (20 November 1858 – 16 March 1940) is a Swedish author (Wikipedia).

Selmathon

The r-ball for Selma Lagerlöf is developed during Selmathon 1 and Selmathon 2 (variations on a Jane-athon) by adding RDA data to the Selma Lagerlöf base r-ball.
