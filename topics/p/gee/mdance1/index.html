Maurice Dance

The first Maurice Dance will take place on 16 Oct 2015 at the National Library of New Zealand, Wellington, New Zealand.

The aim is to build upon the Maurice Gee base r-ball.

  • Scope
  • Announcements, blogs, etc.

Outline

  • 08.30 Registration
  • 09.00 The Maurice Dance
  • 16.30 End

Scope

Resources associated with Maurice Gee are the main theme for the dance, however other New Zealand authors, such as Janet Frame, and Katherine Mansfield could also join the set.

Announcements, blogs, etc.
