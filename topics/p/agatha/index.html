Agatha Christie

Topic: Person: Agatha Christie

Number of entities:

The focus of this r-ball is the English novelist and playwright Agatha Christie (15 September 1890 – 12 January 1976) (Wikipedia). She also created works using the names Mary Westmacott and Agatha Christie Mallowan. It is claimed that she is the best-selling novelist of all time (2 billion items), and the most-translated individual author (expressions in 100+ languages). One of her works has the longest initial run of performances (25000+ in a period of 60+ years). Many of her works have been adapted as graphic novels, motion pictures, radio programs, television programs, video games, and other works (Wikipedia).
