Focus

Works

  • Blade Runner

The focus of an r-ball can be a specific RDA Work.

The r-ball is likely to contain data for:

  • RDA elements describing the Work
  • one or more RDA Expressions that realize the Work
  • one or more RDA Manifestations that embody each Expression
  • other RDA Works, and their Expressions and Manifestations, that are related to the Work
