Jane number

The Jane number of a resource is the number of RDA relationships linking the resource to Jane Austen.

For example:

  • The Jane number of her novel "Emma" is 1: Jane (Person) is creator of Emma (Work).
  • The Jane number of Colin Firth appears to be 3: Jane (Person) is creator of Pride and prejudice (Work) is adapted as television program (work) Pride and Prejudice (Work) has actor Colin Firth (Person).

The Jane number is based on the Bacon number. The Bacon number of an actor or actress is the number of degrees of separation he or she has from Bacon, as defined by the game Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon.
