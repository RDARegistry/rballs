Organization

Topic

Choose a topic for the X-athon.

This acts as a focus for selecting materials for description, and for the set of related entities used to describe and access them.

These entities and relationships can be identified in advance and included in a base r-ball for the X-athon.

>

The topic should support a sufficient range of works, expressions, and manifestations to give participants a choice of simplicity, complexity, familiarity, or specific metadata interest.

Many X-athons have used the works of a single person as a topic. The original "jane-athons" focused on the works of Jane Austen.

Scope

Consider restricting the scope of selected materials to avoid known issues, such as aggregations, or other complexities that will take too much time to explore.
